// Core Modules

What ORION Does

MODULE_01 // JOB_INTEL
Job Search Automation
  • Scans Indeed, LinkedIn & USAJobs RSS feeds continuously
  • Scores postings against your resume automatically
  • Drafts tailored cover letters in your voice
  • Tracks applications, follow-ups & recruiter contacts
  • Flags roles about to expire or repeatedly reposted
MODULE_02 // EMAIL_INT
Email Intelligence
  • Scans Gmail & Outlook every 15 minutes
  • Auto-categorizes: recruiter, urgent, action needed
  • Drafts replies in your voice and tone
  • Surfaces buried leads you forgot about
  • Auto-unsubscribes from junk and marketing
MODULE_03 // CAL_OPS
Calendar & Interview Prep
  • Morning brief: schedule, priorities, top job leads
  • Pre-loads company research before every interview
  • Generates likely questions and talking points
  • Blocks focus time around scheduled commitments
  • Reminds follow-ups from open email threads
MODULE_04 // SOC_PIPE
SOC Security Integration
  • All agent activity piped into Wazuh SOC pipeline
  • Alerts on unexpected behavior or unusual API calls
  • Weekly activity reports — everything ORION did
  • Detects API key misuse & unauthorized access
  • Full audit trail — every action logged & reviewable
MODULE_05 // INTEL_OPS
Intelligence Gathering
  • Researches companies and hiring managers pre-interview
  • Monitors when target companies post new roles
  • Tracks salary trends for your target positions
  • Builds recruiter outreach lists overnight
MODULE_06 // LIFE_OPS
Personal Operations
  • Daily briefing — weather, schedule, priorities
  • GRC compliance deadline monitoring
  • Study session scheduling & exam alerts
  • VPS health and container uptime monitoring

// The Founder

Built by a SOC Analyst who got tired of waiting.

ORION was built out of necessity. After years running security operations across 500+ endpoints, I applied the same systems thinking I use in the SOC to my own job search — and realized the entire process could be automated, monitored, and secured.

Before cybersecurity, I spent 20 years as an executive chef running high-pressure kitchens. Triage. Documentation. Clear communication under stress. Turns out that's exactly what security operations looks like. ORION is what happens when both worlds collide.
